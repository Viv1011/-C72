Places Must Visit

the pagoda

i had visited in october 2017

In Kyoto, Japan there is a stupa-style building called The Porcelain Pagoda of Nanjing.The building seems like it has wings on each for edges. The pagoda is a really religious building for the Japanese. It is definitly a building you have never seen before.

the one world trade center

i had visited in july 2018

The One World Trade Center is one of the tallest buildings in the USA it was actually two buildings before but a plane crashed into them causing many deaths. There is even a fountain labeling all the people who didn't survive. But the One world trade center is beautiful and has an excuisite design.

Review On The Movies

Jurassic World Dominion
This is an amazing movie

I watched this movie in a theatre and it was an amazing experience. The movie had so much action and many new dinosaurs there are a few scenes which jump scare you. The dinosaurs looked so realitic. I even thought that I was in the movie. This also had characters from Jurassic Park which was so crazy. That made the movie experience even better.

No Way Home

The start of this movie is basically the end of the movie Far From Home. This movie had so may stunts and and all of the actors who have acted as spiderman. Overall I think it is the best spiderman movie yet.
